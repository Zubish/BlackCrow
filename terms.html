Terms of Service

These terms describe the expected use of BlackCrow. They should be reviewed by counsel before public launch.

Escrow Role

BlackCrow provides tools for buyers and sellers to define transaction terms, verify access by email, track escrow status, and request release or withdrawal actions.

User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate transaction details, use an email address they control, and only create escrows for lawful, verifiable goods or services.

Funding and Release

Funds should only be treated as escrowed after payment provider confirmation. Buyers release funds after delivery or after agreed conditions are met.

Seller Wallet and Withdrawal

Released funds become available to the seller wallet. A withdrawal request is not final until payout to the seller bank account is marked paid by BlackCrow or its payment provider.

Restricted Use

BlackCrow must not be used for illegal goods, unsafe items, fraudulent transactions, impersonation, money laundering, or transactions that cannot be reasonably verified.

Disputes

If buyer and seller disagree, BlackCrow may review transaction records, acceptance history, funding records, release conditions, and communication supplied by the parties.

Changes

BlackCrow may update these terms as the product, payment partners, and regulatory obligations evolve.
