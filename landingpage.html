How It Works

1. Create link

Buyer or seller starts with names, emails, amount, item, and release condition.

2. Both parties accept

Each party opens the same link and verifies access with their email.

3. Buyer funds

Funds move into escrow hold and stay locked during delivery.

4. Seller delivers

The seller completes the delivery or milestone in the agreed condition.

5. Buyer releases

The buyer confirms the condition and releases funds to the seller wallet.

6. Seller withdraws

Released wallet funds can be withdrawn to a local bank account.

Start From Either Side

I'm buying safely

Create a link when you want protection before paying a seller. The seller accepts the terms, you fund escrow, and you release after delivery.

I'm selling safely
