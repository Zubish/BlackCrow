Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains the data BlackCrow expects to process. It should be reviewed before public launch.

Information We Collect

BlackCrow may collect names, email addresses, escrow details, transaction amounts, item descriptions, acceptance events, payment references, and withdrawal request details.

How We Use Information

We use this information to create escrow links, verify buyer and seller access, process payments, track release conditions, support withdrawals, and help resolve disputes.

Email Verification

Guest access uses email OTP codes so only the attached buyer or seller can act on an escrow.

Payment and Bank Data

Payment processing and bank payout details may be handled by payment partners. BlackCrow should store only the information needed to operate the escrow and payout flow.

Security

BlackCrow uses session tokens, OTP access, server-side validation, and provider webhook verification. Users should still protect their email accounts and avoid sharing access codes.

Retention

Escrow records may be retained for support, dispute review, accounting, fraud prevention, and legal obligations.

Contact

Privacy questions should be sent to support@blackcrow.com until a dedicated privacy address is configured.
