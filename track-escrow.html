Track and act on an escrow

Enter the escrow ID and the buyer or seller email attached to the transaction. BlackCrow generates a short access code before unlocking the actions that apply to you.

Buyer actions

Accept terms, initialize simulated payment, confirm funding, and release funds after delivery.

Seller actions

Accept terms, mark delivery complete, and withdraw released wallet funds to a local bank.
